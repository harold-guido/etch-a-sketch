INSTRUCTIONS

This is a canvas you can draw on a canvas using different pens:
- the "Normal" pen lets you draw in balck ink
- the "Rainbow" pen lets you draw in rainbow ink (which randomly changes colours as you draw)
- the "Shades of Black" pen adds a darker shade of black to a pixel of the grid each time it is drawn over the "Normal" pen lets you draw in balck ink
- the "Eraser" pen lets you draw in white ink


Additionally:
- the "Resize" button lets you determine the new number of pixels per side of the canvas
